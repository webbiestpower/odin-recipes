Coconut cheese pie recipe

Description

This pie is for you if you’re as addicted to coconut as I am. A Coconut Chess Pie is a baked coconut pie that’s creamy like custard and FULL of coconut flavor. It’s like eating a macaroon with a pudding base and a pie crust chaser. *swoon*

Ingredient

  • 1 all butter pie crust or from a refrigerated pack of two, or frozen
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon all purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Place pie crust in a 9” pie plate and crimp edges as desired. Chill until ready to fill.
  2. Whisk the eggs, then whisk in the flour and sugar until smooth. Whisk in the melted butter, both extracts, salt and the buttermilk. Stir in 2 cups of the coconut.
